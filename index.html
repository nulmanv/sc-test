Security Policy

Privacy Preservation

  • Do not collect more personal data than necessary
  • Encrypt sensitive information both at rest and in transit
  • Limit access to user data to authorized personnel only
  • Regularly review and purge outdated or unnecessary data
  • Comply with applicable privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA)

General Security Guidelines

  • Do not allow tailgating into secure areas
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep fire exits clear at all times
  • Wear visible ID badges on premises
